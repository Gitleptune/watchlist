Notes

Info

  • A dial is the shell of a dead shellfish
  • If the shell remains intact, a dial should work even after 200 years
  • Skypiea civilization wouldn’t function without dials
  • There are dial-craft engineers
  • Dial ships
    • Karasumaru A ship slower than a waver that has 2 wind dials.
  • Users can use a Reject dial once or twice before they die

Dial Crafts

  • Impact Dial + Glove/Bandage = Weapon that can kill people
  • Heat Dial + Spear = Heat Javelin
  • Flame Dial + Bird (inside its beak) = Fire-Breathing Bird
  • Wind Dial + Gas + Bazooka = Burn Bazooka, Burns everything in its way with a small flame
  • Jet Dial + Waver = Even Faster Waver
